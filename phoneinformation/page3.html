Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra vs iPhone 15 Pro Max

This in-depth comparison analyzes the Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra and the iPhone 15 Pro Max, two of the most anticipated flagship phones of the year. We've examined their key features using the criteria outlined in our "Phone Specs & Features" page.

Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra - $1,299

  • Display: 6.8-inch Dynamic AMOLED, 1440 x 3088 pixels, 120Hz adaptive refresh rate, 501ppi, Gorilla Glass Armor
  • Processor: Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 (for Galaxy)
  • Camera: 200MP main camera, 5x optical zoom, 12MP ultrawide, 12MP telephoto, 50MP telephoto
  • Battery: 5000mAh, 45W fast charging, wireless charging, reverse wireless charging
  • Storage: 256GB, 512GB, 1TB, UFS 4.0

iPhone 15 Pro Max - $1,199

  • Display: 6.7-inch OLED, 1290 x 2796 pixels, 120Hz adaptive refresh rate, 460ppi, Ceramic Shield
  • Processor: Apple A17 Pro
  • Camera: 48MP main camera, 5x optical zoom, 12MP ultrawide, 12MP telephoto, depth sensor
  • Battery: 4441mAh, 23W fast charging, wireless charging
  • Storage: 256GB, 512GB, 1TB, NVMe

In terms of display, both phones offer stunning visuals with high resolutions and adaptive refresh rates. However, the Galaxy S24 Ultra boasts a slightly larger display and a higher pixel density, resulting in sharper details. The iPhone 15 Pro Max, on the other hand, features Ceramic Shield, providing superior scratch resistance.

The processor battle is intense. The Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 in the Galaxy S24 Ultra delivers exceptional performance, while the Apple A17 Pro in the iPhone 15 Pro Max consistently excels in benchmarks. The choice here depends on your priorities: raw processing power or a balanced experience across different tasks.

The camera systems on both phones are top-notch. The Galaxy S24 Ultra boasts a 200MP main sensor, enabling incredibly detailed photos, while the iPhone 15 Pro Max excels in its overall image quality and dynamic range. The Galaxy S24 Ultra also features a more versatile camera system with multiple telephoto lenses, offering superior zoom capabilities.

Battery life is another key consideration. While the Galaxy S24 Ultra has a larger battery capacity, the iPhone 15 Pro Max consistently achieves longer battery life in real-world usage. Both phones support fast charging, though the Galaxy S24 Ultra charges significantly faster.

Both phones offer ample storage options, with the Galaxy S24 Ultra using UFS 4.0 technology, which offers faster read and write speeds. However, the iPhone 15 Pro Max relies on NVMe storage, a technology known for its durability and performance.

Ultimately, the best phone for you depends on your individual needs and preferences. The Galaxy S24 Ultra excels in display quality, camera versatility, and fast charging, while the iPhone 15 Pro Max offers a well-rounded experience with exceptional performance, a durable design, and excellent battery life.
