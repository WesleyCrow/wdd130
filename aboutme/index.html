Dream Job

My dream is to become a therapist who uses technology to enhance the therapeutic experience. Telehealth and online therapy platforms are becoming increasingly popular, and I believe they can be incredibly powerful tools for making mental healthcare more accessible and convenient. Imagine using VR therapy to help people with phobias confront their fears in a safe, controlled environment. Or using AI-powered emotional recognition to provide early support and resources to those struggling with mild anxiety or depression. The possibilities are exciting, and I'm eager to see how technology can continue to shape the future of therapy.
