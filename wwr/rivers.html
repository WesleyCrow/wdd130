Our River Adventures

Choose your perfect river experience. From gentle floats to thrilling rapids, we have something for every skill level and preference.

Beginner: Cumberland River, Kentucky

The Cumberland River is perfect for beginners and families. Open from May to September, this dam-controlled river offers reliable flows and a great introduction to whitewater rafting. The difficulty level ranges from Class II to Class III, making it an ideal choice for those new to the sport. You will enjoy the scenic beauty of the southern landscape and the refreshing relief from the humidity as well.

Intermediate: Ocoee River, Tennessee

The Ocoee River is a fantastic choice for intermediate rafters. Known for its Class III and Class IV rapids, this river offers a thrilling adventure with moderate to intense waves and drops. The Ocoee is famous for hosting the 1996 Olympic whitewater events, adding a touch of history to your rafting experience. This features exhilarating rapids and the stunning natural surroundings of the Tennessee landscape.

Expert: Hudson River Gorge, New York

The Hudson River Gorge is a top destination for expert rafters. Rated as one of the top ten whitewater rafting trips in the U.S., this river features Class IV and V rapids, providing a challenging and adrenaline-pumping experience. The 15-mile stretch of whitewater is combined with spectacular scenery, making it a memorable adventure for seasoned rafters. Experience the powerful rapids and the breathtaking views of the gorge.
